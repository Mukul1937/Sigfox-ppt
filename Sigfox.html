UNB Technology

Ultra-Narrowband (UNB) radio modulation enables small data packets transmission over extended distances with minimal power consumption.

Section 3 Technical Specifications

Technical Specifications

-ISM Bands
Industrial, Scientific, Medical bands - globally available, no licensing required. 868 MHz for Europe/Africa/Asia, 902 MHz for Americas.
-Ultra-Narrowband
Only 100 Hz bandwidth per signal enables high power density, excellent noise immunity, and extended range compared to wideband alternatives.
-Regional Variants
RC1 (Europe/Africa/Asia): 100 bps
RC2 (Americas): 600 bps
Higher rates in Americas due to different spectrum regulations.
-12 Byte Payload
Sufficient for sensor values (temp, location, status), GPS coordinates (~8 bytes), or simple telemetry. Requires efficient encoding.

Section 8

Conclusion

Sigfox has established itself as a foundational technology in the LPWAN ecosystem, demonstrating that purpose-built IoT connectivity delivers significant advantages over adapted technologies.

The technology excels in specific use cases: simple sensors transmitting small amounts of data infrequently over long distances while operating on battery power for extended periods.
